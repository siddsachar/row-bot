# Code of Conduct

Row-Bot is a personal-AI-sovereignty project. The community should be useful,
direct, curious, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent, especially with new contributors.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and its users.
- Respect privacy. Do not post another person's private information.
- Be patient when helping someone learn the codebase.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted sexual attention.
- Hate speech or discriminatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Trolling, repeated derailment, or bad-faith participation.
- Any conduct that makes the project unsafe or unwelcoming.

## Enforcement

Reports can be sent privately to siddsachar@gmail.com.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other
reasonable action to protect the community. Enforcement decisions are made in
the interest of keeping the project safe, constructive, and legally healthy.
